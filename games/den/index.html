Den

A Sega Master System Roguelike

Proof of concept game vibe-coded in Z80 Assembly using Claude Opus 4.6. Graphics and sound also nearly all AI generated (for now). To play, pick your race, starting items, and then take the staircase down. On the 5th level is the boss - the honey badger.

Controls

ARROWS Move
A Menu / Confirm
S Wait / Cancel
